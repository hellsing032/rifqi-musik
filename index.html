About

I am a percussionist, arranger, and composer based in Bandung, Indonesia, with a background in music education from the Indonesian University of Education. I have been involved in the field of music for 6 years.

Currently, I do not have any original composition works as they are still in the process of creation. My musical abilities include composing in various genres such as orchestral, big band, popular music, contemporary music, percussion ensemble compositions, and more.

With my passion and talent in the field of music, I am committed to engaging in any musical endeavors that contribute to the advancement of music worldwide.
